# USER_ROLES.md

## Overview

EduNexus uses a Role-Based Access Control (RBAC) system.

A single user may have multiple roles.

Example:

* Teacher
* Class Teacher
* Hostel Master
* Science Club Patron

can all belong to the same person.

---

# Administration Roles

## Principal

The overall head of the school.

Responsibilities:

* Manage school operations
* View all reports
* Approve academic records
* Monitor attendance
* Monitor finance performance
* Manage staff

---

## Deputy Principal

Assists the Principal in managing school operations.

Responsibilities:

* Manage discipline
* Monitor attendance
* Manage academic activities
* View reports

---

## Director

Overall head of an institution or school network.

Responsibilities:

* Manage multiple schools
* View institution-wide reports
* Strategic planning

---

## Deputy Director

Assists the Director.

Responsibilities:

* Operational oversight
* Staff supervision

---

## Registrar

Responsible for student records.

Responsibilities:

* Student admissions
* Student transfers
* Student registration
* Student profile management

---

## ICT Administrator

Responsible for system administration.

Responsibilities:

* Manage user accounts
* Manage permissions
* Technical support
* System configuration

---

# Academic Roles

## Teacher

Responsible for teaching assigned subjects.

Responsibilities:

* Record attendance
* Enter grades
* Communicate with students
* Communicate with parents

---

## Class Teacher

A teacher assigned to a specific class.

Responsibilities:

* Monitor class attendance
* Track student welfare
* Communicate with parents
* Generate class reports

---

## Head of Department (HOD)

Responsible for an academic department.

Responsibilities:

* Supervise teachers
* Review performance
* Coordinate departmental activities

---

## Guidance and Counselling Teacher

Responsible for student welfare.

Responsibilities:

* Student counselling
* Career guidance
* Student support

---

# Student Roles

---

---

## Class Prefect

Class representative.

---

# Parent & Guardian Roles

## Parent

Responsible for a student.

Responsibilities:

* Monitor attendance
* Monitor grades
* Pay fees
* Receive announcements
* Communicate with teachers

---

## Guardian

Acts on behalf of a parent.

Responsibilities:

* Similar permissions to a parent
