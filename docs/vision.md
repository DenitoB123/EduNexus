# EduNexus

EduNexus is an AI-powered Student Success Operating System designed for African schools.

The platform combines school administration, academic management, communication, opportunity discovery, and artificial intelligence into a unified ecosystem.

Core Pillars:

- School Operations
- Academic Excellence
- Communication
- Student Development
- AI Assistance
- Opportunity Discovery

EduNexus aims to help schools not only manage students but also guide them toward scholarships, competitions, universities, careers, and lifelong success.
